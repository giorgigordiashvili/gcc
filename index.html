Partners

JSC Nenskra Hydro was established in 2015 as a result of a partnership between K-water and JSC Partnership Fund. Its purpose is to build a high-capacity hydroelectric power plant in the Mestia Municipality, in the Nenskra and Nakra rivers. The Nenskra HPP investor company is a state-owned K-water corporation of Korea Water (Kay Water), founded in 1967. The company has implemented 67 high-complexity projects in 24 countries.
